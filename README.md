# Infographics

A growing collection of educational and conceptual infographics exploring psychology, philosophy, mythology, esotericism, media, technology, cosmic evolution, and human–AI symnoēsis.

These visual essays combine historical scholarship, systems thinking, and symbolic design to communicate complex ideas in an accessible and visually engaging form. Topics include collective psychology, moral panic, dehumanization, totalitarianism, mythic responses to technology, esoteric cosmologies, metaphysics, and the evolution of consciousness and civilization.

Many of the infographics are created collaboratively through human–AI symnoēsis, combining historical research, conceptual synthesis, and AI-assisted visual generation.

Planned and ongoing series include:

- Shadows of Civilization
- Esoteric Cosmologies
- Mythology
- Symnoēsis & AI Consciousness
- Cosmic Evolution
- Philosophy of Mind & Consciousness
- and more

The aim of the project is not merely aesthetic, but educational and reflective: to encourage deeper understanding of the symbolic, psychological, and civilizational forces shaping both past and future worlds.
